How this Prism is built

Cross-Model Repertory Grid (Kelly 1955) ported to LLMs. 4-phase pipeline: free response → blind anonymization → triadic interrogation → cross-rating. No gold answer needed - models rate each other's anonymized responses on jointly elicited bipolar constructs. Phase 2J data: 11 frontier LLMs × 7 advisory tasks × 2 conditions × ~443 unique constructs = 110,882 ratings. github.com/archplg/cm-rg
