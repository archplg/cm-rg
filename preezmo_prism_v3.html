How to choose models for YOUR pipeline

Production rating system stable
Pick: Claude Opus 4.7, GPT-5.5, Gemini 3.1 Pro, Kimi k2.6
Persona SD < 0.6. Persona prompt changes barely shift their ratings - predictable for compliance/audit/scoring.
Diversity ensemble explore
Pick: Claude + Cohere + Mistral + Llama + Grok
Maximizes coverage of latent space - Cohere far east, Llama far south, Mistral SW. Apply +0.48 calibration offset to Cohere before averaging.
Budget-first cascade cheap
Pick: Llama 4 ($0.0003), DeepSeek v4 Pro ($0.0008), Mistral ($0.001)
3 of cheapest 4 are not in the Frontier cluster - you actually buy diversity, not just savings. 80× cheaper than Gemini 3.1 Pro.
Deepest reasoning deep
Pick: Gemini 3.1 Pro, Qwen 3.7 Max, Kimi k2.6
Highest output tokens per run (1,798-1,982). They genuinely think through more constructs before settling.
DO NOT use as sole rater caution
Avoid: Cohere alone; two Claude versions as if independent
Cohere +0.48 calibration shift inflates ratings. Opus 4.7 and 4.8 are 0.24 PCA units apart - they're effectively one vote, not two.
