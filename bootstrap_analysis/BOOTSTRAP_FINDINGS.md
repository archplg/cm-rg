## How to read

- **CI excludes 0** -> effect is reliably non-zero in the bootstrapped distribution.
- **CI width** = uncertainty. Tight CI = stable estimate. Wide CI = need more data.
- **Procrustes < 0.3** -> Platonic Hypothesis supported. **> 0.3** -> rejected. CI tells you the conclusion's robustness.
- **Variance decomposition**: each factor's share of total rating variance. Higher = bigger driver.
