How this Prism is built

Method
Cross-Model Repertory Grid
Triadic construct elicitation + cross-rating. Adapted from George Kelly's Personal Construct Psychology (1955).
Pipeline
4-phase, fully automated
Free response → anonymization (E1-E11 labels) → triadic interrogation → cross-rating on 1-7 scale.
Anonymization
Yes, blind cross-rating
No model sees the authorship of any response it rates. Eliminates brand-based bias (Sun et al., 2026).
Variance explained
PC1 + PC2 = 55.6%
PC1 captures 34.8% of variance, PC2 captures 20.8%. Remaining 44.4% in higher dimensions, not shown.
Sample
3,987 ratings per model
14 cells (7 advisory tasks × 2 conditions), 11 frontier LLMs, ~443 constructs. Snapshot: May 2026.
Contamination resistance
No gold answer
Unlike SWE-Bench or MMLU, there is no "correct" rating a model could memorize or extract from the environment.
